Dear patients and visitors, here you can find information for international / forieng patients.

Our International Department is located directly in the Clinic Solingen, and it was founded especially for the patients from all over the world.

Our office is the administrative center for patients from around the world. Our staff is ready to provide You with information about the medical services of the hospital, Solingen and assist you in organising Your treatment. Our consulting center are ready to provide you with all needed support and information according to:

  • appointment and consultation,
  • diagnostics,
  • inpatient and outpatient treatment.

International Department helps you with translation of medical documents and records, visa support, transfers from airport to the hospital and back, accomodation near clinic, inpatient and outpatient treatment and many other.

We totally understand that sometimes the processes of diagnostics and treatment might be stressful for the patient and the relatives, family members. That is why we are here to support you and to make all the procedures easier and comfortable.

You can be sure, in Solingen City Hospital you will be in the reliable hands of professional doctors and medical personnel.

Additional services:
