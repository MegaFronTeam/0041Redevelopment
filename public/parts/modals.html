Neurology

The Neurosurgery at the hospital Solingen is one of the leading neurosurgical centres in Europe. Firmly anchored in the Neurocentre of the Head Clinic and the neighbouring specialist clinics of the University Medical School, we offer our patients neurosurgical therapy at the highest medical level. In five state-of-the-art operating theatres, we treat the entire spectrum of diseases of the peripheral and central nervous system. Special focal points are brain tumour surgery and spinal surgery.
